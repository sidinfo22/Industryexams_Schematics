Our Background

Industry Exams Plus was founded during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic on the East Coast, bringing together a diverse group of undergraduate students from majors like IT, Computer Science, Nursing, Finance, and more.

What started as an individual project soon evolved into a mission to uplift the community, providing resources for peers to study effectively and achieve their career goals.

Many of the contributors to Industry Exams Plus are experienced professionals who have successfully attained the certifications featured on our platform. Drawing from their expertise across diverse fields such as nursing, IT, finance, and tech support, our team ensures that the resources we provide are both accurate and practical, reflecting real-world industry standards.
